# Product Guidelines

## Visual Language & UX
- **Theme:** High-tech, futuristic, holographic aesthetics.
- **Palette:** Dark mode as default. Accent colors should be vibrant neon hues (electric blue, cyan, magenta) to simulate a holographic effect.
- **Typography:** Sans-serif, modern, highly legible. Support for monospaced fonts for technical data.
- **Interactions:** Smooth, physics-based transitions (using Framer Motion and Three.js animations).
- **3D Standards:** Optimized low-poly models where possible, with high-quality PBR textures for key assets (helmets, logos).

## Content & Tone
- **Voice:** Professional, strategic, and concise.
- **Tone:** Encouraging but data-driven. The "Nexus" persona should feel like an elite co-pilot.
- **Clarity:** Technical football and AI terms should be clearly explained or visually contextualized.

## Technical Standards
- **Performance:** Maintain a minimum of 60fps for the 3D viewport.
- **Responsiveness:** Ensure critical data is accessible on mobile, though the full 3D experience is optimized for desktop.
- **Code Style:** Strict TypeScript typing. Component-based architecture with React.
