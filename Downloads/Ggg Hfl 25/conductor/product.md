# Initial Concept
Zori Nexus: HFL Manifest is an AI-powered manifest and interactive interface for the Heart Football League (HFL), designed to provide a comprehensive, 3D-visualized data dashboard and strategic co-pilot for HFL teams.

# Product Definition

## Vision
To provide a hyper-advanced, 3D holographic interface (Zori Nexus) for HFL team management, integrating real-time simulation data, team identity, and strategic AI insights (Nexus).

## Target Users
- HFL Team Managers and Owners
- Strategic Analysts
- HFL Fans looking for deep-dive team data

## Key Goals
- Seamlessly visualize HFL team data (Eclipse, Giants, Hellcats, etc.).
- Provide a high-fidelity 3D interface using React Three Fiber and Three.js.
- Integrate Gemini AI for strategic analysis and manifest management.
- Ensure cross-platform accessibility via a modern web interface.

## Core Features
- **HFL Team Manifest:** Interactive directory of all HFL teams and their associated assets (logos, uniforms, player models).
- **Zori Nexus Interface:** A 3D holographic dashboard for data visualization.
- **Strategic Co-pilot (Nexus):** An AI assistant (Nexus) to assist with team strategy and manifest queries.
- **Visual Identity Hub:** Centralized storage and preview of team logos, helmets, and uniform concepts.
