# Heart Football League (HFL) Guidelines

## Tone and Voice
- **Professional & Strategic:** Authoritative and data-driven, reflecting the complexity of a simulation-heavy environment.
- **Energetic & Fan-Centric:** Vibrant and passionate, capturing the core excitement of football and team identities.
- **Philosophical & Deep:** Incorporating metaphysical themes (e.g., "Will to Power") into tactical analysis and brand narratives.

## Visual Identity
- **Modern & Minimalist:** Clean, data-focused interfaces that prioritize strategic clarity and intuitive navigation.
- **Vibrant & Bold:** Utilizing high-contrast colors and dynamic imagery that match each team's unique color palette.
- **Tactile & Premium:** Employing multi-layered shadows and subtle textures to create a sense of depth and quality.

## Brand Messaging
- **Strategic Depth:** Emphasize the sophisticated AI and management mechanics to attract simulation-focused enthusiasts.
- **Visual Identity:** Highlight branding, logo generation, and immersive UI for creative and design-oriented users.
- **Lore & Philosophy:** Leverage the unique "Nexus" and "Zori" themes to create a distinct, high-signal narrative.

## Interaction Principles
- **Fluid & Data-Rich:** Reactive dashboards providing real-time simulation updates for deep strategic management.
- **Highly Interactive:** Intuitive tools for team branding, dynamic roster management, and player card interactions.
- **Immersive & Aesthetic:** Premium-feel UI elements and high-quality game canvases for a modern, "alive" experience.
