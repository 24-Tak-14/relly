# Heart Football League (HFL)

## Product Vision
The Heart Football League is a hyper-advanced football simulation ecosystem that merges sophisticated AI-driven strategy with deep sports management mechanics and an interactive visual frontend. It aims to provide a "living" football universe where every tactical choice and management decision has a measurable impact on the simulation's outcome.

## Target Audience
- **Football Enthusiasts & Gamers:** Users seeking a deep, simulation-style management experience that goes beyond surface-level statistics.
- **Developers & Hobbyists:** Individuals interested in exploring sports simulation logic, AI coaching systems, and modern web technologies like React, Three.js, and Firebase.

## Primary Goals
- **Immersive Simulation:** Creating a complex game engine that utilizes dice-based systems and AI coaching to simulate realistic football outcomes.
- **Interactive Branding & Management:** Empowering users with tools for dynamic team identity creation (logo generation) and detailed roster management (player cards).
- **Comprehensive Management Ecosystem:** Building a robust backend for monetization, contract negotiations, injury tracking, and league-wide logistics.
- **Scalable Architecture:** Developing a modular codebase that supports expansion into different platforms and advanced gameplay features.

## Key Features
- **AI Coaching & Game Logic:** A sophisticated engine that manages on-field decision-making and strategic transitions.
- **Visual Game Canvas:** A React and Three.js based interface for visualizing matches and managing team assets.
- **Deep Front Office Systems:** Integrated systems for contracts, finance, media relations, and player personnel management.
- **Dynamic Asset Generation:** Automated tools for creating team logos and player profiles based on league data.
